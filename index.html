These cards are the ultimate infinite enablers. The biggest offenders are the common rarity cards—Pommel Strike and Bloodletting. Just with these two cards you have infinite. It’s far too easy to force this build in every run. It stops being a "lucky find" and becomes a chore to not do it. Once you realize you can just thin the deck and click the same two cards until the boss dies, the strategic depth of a deck builder basically evaporates. It turns a tactical roguelike into a clicking simulator.
